About Me

I AM 'RESULT ORIENTED', But I am also a really fun person which makes for a wonderful harmony of these two very important aspects of life. Having started my journey in tech ( software engineering in particular ) a little shy of two years now, I understand that there is so much more to learn, to build and to achieve and this excites me a lot as I look to keep moving forward and improving myself in this line of work. I studied Law at Nnamdi Azikwe University, Awka, but decided to make a career switch to software engineering because I believe strongly that this is the future of the world and I do not wish to be left behind as this new age is fully ushered in. This decision to make this career change should tell you a bit about my resolve and determination in achieving my objectives. Some months back I got enrolled in the Hackathon '22 AfricaPlan Foundation Programme where I am being trained as a MERN fullstack developer. I have been exposed to a great deal of knowledge while working on a couple of capstone projects. Something that I love about software development is the opportunity to work with people from a wide range of disciplines as I absolutely relish this and thrive in this environment. The software development lifecycle involves working with so many different people and departments and, as a people person and someone who loves to build new relationships, it feels great to be able to come together with other workers who are striving towards the same goal. I am always great to work with and I get my tasks done before their deadlines
